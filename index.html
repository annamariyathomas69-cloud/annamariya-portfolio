About Me

I'm Anna Mariya Thomas, a passionate 2nd-year Cybersecurity student at Jyothi Engineering College, Thrissur. My journey in the field of cybersecurity is driven by a deep commitment to understanding and mitigating cyber threats in an increasingly digital world.

Beyond academics, I am an avid speaker and enthusiast of various activities and research initiatives.
